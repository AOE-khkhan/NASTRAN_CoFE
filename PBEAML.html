PBEAML

Defines the properties of a beam element by cross-sectional dimensions.

Format

123456789
PBEAMLPIDMIDGROUPTYPE
DIM1DIM2-etc.-NSM

Example

PBEAML,101,501,,BAR,,,,,
,0.031291,0.181177,0.002854
PBEAML,102,501,,ROD,,,,,
,0.015135,0.0

Fields

Field Contents
PID Property identification number. (Integer > 0)
MID Material identification number. (Integer > 0)
GROUP Not used.
TYPE Cross-section shape. See Remark 1. (Character: ``ROD'' or ``BAR'')
DIMi Cross-section dimensions. (Real > 0.0)
NSM Nonstructural mass per unit length. (Real, Default = 0.0)

Remarks:

  1. Cross-sections dimensions are shown in Figs. 1 and 2.
