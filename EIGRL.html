EIGRL

Defines data needed to perform real eigenvalue (vibration or buckling) analysis.

Format

123456789
EIGRLSIDV1V2NDMSGLVLMAXSETSHFSCLNORM

Example

EIGRL,1,,,10

Fields

Field Contents
SID Set identification number. (Unique Integer > 0)
V1, V2 Not used.
ND Number of roots desired. (Integer > 0)
MSGLVL, MAXSET, SHFSCL, NORM Not used.

Remarks:

  1. Vibration eigenvectors are mass normalized.
  2. Buckling eigenvectors are elastic-stiffness normalized.
