MAT1

Defines the material properties for linear isotropic materials.

Format

123456789
MAT1MIDEGNURHOATREFGE

Example

MAT1,501,1440000000.0,,0.33,5.370788

Fields

Field Contents
MID Material identification number. (Integer > 0)
E Young's modulus. (Real, Required)
G Shear modulus. See Remark 1. (Real > 0.0 or blank)
NU Poisson's ratio. See Remark 1. (Real or blank)
RHO Mass density. (Real, Default = 0.0)
A Not used.
TREF Not used.
GE Not used.

Remarks:

  1. Either NU or G may be omitted. The other will be calculated using the following relationship: $$G = \frac{E}{2(1+NU)}$$
