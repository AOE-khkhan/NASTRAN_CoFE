PSHELL

Defines the properties of membrane elements. In the near future, the entry will be expanded to support bending and transverse shear properties of thin shell elements.

Format

123456789
PSHELLPIDMID1TMID212I/T**3MID3TS/TNSM
Z1Z2MID4

Example

PSHELL,105,501,0.0833,501,,501,,4.0

Fields

Field Contents
PID Property identification number. (Integer > 0)
MID1 Material identification number for the membrane. (Integer > 0 or blank)
T Membrane thickness. (Real)
MID2, 12I/T**3, MID3, TS/T, NSM, Z1, Z2, MID4 Not used.

Remarks:

  1. The continuation is optional.
